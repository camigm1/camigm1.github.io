Website Planning

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide information about the city of Lehi,Utah and it's surrounding businesses and attractions to newcomers or people looking to see what amenities the city provides. The site is supposed to unify the businesses in the community updating on current events and organizations in the community.

Target Audience

Persona One

The first audience member is those coming to conferences here in Lehi, Utah. Lehi is a booming city with many big companies established here especially around the tech sector and many other businesses. A lot of the people who are coming, are doing so through conferences or they work in the industry of tech and other buinesses in the area. They'll be probably around their 30s-60s, looking for a place to stay a coupld of days and wondering what places they can visit while in their stay. I think the chamber of commerce should give them a good idea on what the city of Lehi is about and what kind of events we hold.
