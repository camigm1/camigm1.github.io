Our purpose is to welcome you and show you what the city of Lehi has to offer to you and our local businesses. Throughout the years we've been a thriving city with astounding development. Adobe

Adobe is one of our featured companies. Top in digital trends and resources to millions of people around the world, Adobe is one of the main buildings that stands out from the I-15. From photoshop to photography, they can make any picture come to life. Definitely on our featured Fortune 500 companies that are headquartered in our growing city. It has 21,428 employees and originally headquartered in San Jose california. if you want to find out more visit our Website.

Micron Technology

An American producer of computer memory and data storage. This is one of the major companies located in Lehi, Utah. Headquartered in Boise, Idaho it was founded in 1978 with $21.4 Billion in revenue. It also has 31,400 employees.Visit our Website.

Another Fortune 500 company and technology with 137,000 employees. Oracle focuses on computer software. Headquartered in California, it has one of the most diverse body of employees. It's company motto "To help people see data in new ways, discover insights, unlock endless possibilities." Visit us at our Website.
