Our History

  • Founded in 1852 by Mormon Pioneers
  • Population of 54,382
  • According to the census, Lehi is the fifth fastest growing city in the country
  • Quickly becoming a technology and commercial center along the Wasatch Front
  • Home of museums, botanical gardens, shopping, and many hotels
