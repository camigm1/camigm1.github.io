Target Audience

My target audience are people that specifically live in Idaho around the ages of 20-45 who are looking to find regional news and weather in their area. They are mainly looking at the weather to enjoy outside activities such as hiking, sports, walking, boating etc. Obviously, people that are older or younger can access the site but it is geared more torwards that more physically active population.

Personas

The first persona that can use this site is a 20 year old male college student who are wanting to make the most out of their college experience by going out with their friends on hikes or even parties. Enjoy a nice ride on the boat by the lake or simply try to take up a new hobby such as running. Either way, as an active college student wanting to go out and enjoy what the area has to offer, they will need to access this site pretty often and have it be a trustworthy part of their college life.

The second type of persona is the one that is in their late 30s, early 40s who is already married and has two kids. She is wanting to have her kids have good experiences and enjoy life and as such they like to plan activities and things to do outside. Maybe visit the local farm with baby animals or go to the new cool playground around the corner. They want to find the awesome acitivites to do together as a family.

Scenarios

The first scenario involves a guy named Tim and his 3 roommates. They have decided to to do something for the weekend which will involve going jet skiing to the local lake and after a couple of hours being in the water they want to go on a hike. As such, they want quick and clear information about what the weather will be like all of that day. This is when they will go to Weather Hub and plan accordingly. While browsing Weather Hub they will also come across local activities and happenings. Even articles on cool hikes in the area. As such, they will find this website very useful to their needs.

The second scenario involves a group of people in their early 40s who are organizing a outdoor yoga event. They include both male and female. They are planning on doing it outside of the yoga teacher's house around 11 am. They will also have food and drinks afterward around afternoon time. As such they will need to look up the weather for the morning and afternoon. The will also be able to find local events happening such as other yoga events in the area. The page will also have other activities that could appeal to early 40s active people who love doing yoga.

The third scenario includes a 30 year old male who likes to boulder around the mountainous area. Him and his 2 close friends who also share a passion for bouldering get together, usually at 8am in the morning to practive bouldering. They like something simple and to the point to get their weather information from and Weather Hub provides that more minimalistic look for what's around the area.

The fourth scenario includes a family of five whose son turns eight. They are planning on having a big birthday party on their backyard and are planning on inviting their kid's friends. They are also planning on having a bbq in the back so knowing if it's gonna be windy or not will be a big factor. AS such they will check the weather site to see what the wind gusts will look like as well as the weather. They will also check if there are any birthday party stores around the area since the weather site will provide all of that information for them.

The fifth scenario is for someone named Marie who is wanting to be more active but hasn't gotten quite there yet. She checks the Weather Hub regularly just to see if there's any activity that she's interested in doing around her community. She wants to take a chance doing more of the community service and eventually getting around to the more physical rigorous activities such as hiking. Maybe checking out the site and seeing the beautiful pictures of nature might give her more encouragement.
